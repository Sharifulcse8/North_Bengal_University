About North Bengal University

Excellence in Education, Research and Innovation Since 2005

Our History

North Bengal University was established in 2005 with a vision to provide quality higher education.

Over the past two decades, the university has grown into one of the leading private universities in Bangladesh, offering undergraduate and graduate programs across six academic departments.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality education, develop innovative research, encourage creativity, and prepare skilled graduates for the global workforce.

  • Quality Education
  • Innovative Research
  • Skilled Graduates
  • Global Workforce

Our Vision

To become a nationally and internationally recognized university through excellence in teaching, research, innovation, and community service.

  • National Recognition
  • International Recognition
  • Excellence in Teaching
  • Research & Innovation

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical practices in all our academic and administrative activities.

Innovation

We encourage creativity, new ideas, and innovative approaches to teaching, learning, and research.

Excellence

We maintain high academic standards and strive for excellence in every aspect of university life.

Leadership

We prepare future leaders through quality education, character development, and community engagement.
